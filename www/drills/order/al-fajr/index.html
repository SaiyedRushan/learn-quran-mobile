Surah 89 · 30 verses
Order the verses — Al-Fajr

Each round deals 4 consecutive verses of this passage, shuffled — with a couple of decoy verses mixed in, some drawn from just before or after the run in this same surah and some from other surahs. Arrange the real ones in the order they are recited, and leave the impostors in the bank. Only the Arabic and transliteration are shown.

  • Tap a card to add it as the next verse — or drag cards to reorder or insert between others.
  • Tap a placed card (or drag it back down) to return it to the bank.
  • 💡 Hint shows the passage's English translation in the correct order — if you know some of the Arabic words, match the cards against it. No penalty.
  • Once the sequence is full, Enter checks — and again moves to the next round.
